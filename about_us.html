About Us

By: Woody Goss, with help from Gemini AI


Team 236 Techno-Ticks, is a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team based in Old Lyme, Connecticut. Founded in 1999, our team is dedicated to fostering a love of science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics (STEAM) in our members and community.


Our Mission

  • To inspire and educate students in the fields of STEM.
  • To foster a culture Gracious-Professionalism
  • To compete at the highest level of FIRST Robotics Competition.
  • To give back to our community through outreach and mentorship.

Team 236 is proud to be a Hall of Fame team since 2009!
